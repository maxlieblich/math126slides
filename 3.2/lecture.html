Angles: example

The angle $\theta$ between $\langle 1,3,5\rangle$ and $\langle -1,3,2\rangle$ satisfies

$$18=\sqrt{35}\sqrt{14}\cos(\theta).$$

Conclusion: $\theta=0.6212404....$ radians, or around $35.59$ degrees.

Angles: orthogonality
